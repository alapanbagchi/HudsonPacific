About Us

Hudson Pacific Property is a Sydney based property advisory firm specialising in the Residential Development market. Hudson Pacific Property has provided strategic advise to diverse range of clients across the residential property development market, corporate property firms and high net worth individuals.

Our Principal, Curtis Field, has over 25 years experience in the property market with a broad base of experience with a focus on residential development. We partner with our clients to ensure the best results and advise – we are an extension of our clients team and always represent them with honesty and integrity. Our clients use Hudson Pacific Property because they know we understand their needs and they respect our experience and advice.

Curtis Field

Curtis has over 25 years experience in the property industry with a specialisation in residential property development, sales and marketing. He has a broad base of experience in other markets including accommodation, leisure and retirement living, retail and commercial and enjoys a broad industry network.

Curtis’ unique skillset includes;
  • A career including Valuation, Advisory, Acquisition, Site Sales and Project Marketing.
  • An in-depth knowledge of the residential development industry across Land Subdivisions, Medium and High Rise apartment developments.
  • A focus on high end and luxury projects.
  • Solid knowledge of the Golf Course, Accommodation, Retirement, Commercial and Retail markets.
  • Involvement in creating the marketing strategy, launch and sales process over $5BN worth of new projects, with a focus on high end projects and $1BN in site sales and acquisitions.
  • International project marketing experience in Asia with connections in key other markets - Middle East, USA & UK.
  • An in depth understanding of the development business including acquisition, valuation, feasibility, financing, sales and marketing, construction, project and development management.
  • Strong knowledge of building and construction methods, costs and general benchmarks within the development industry.
  • Management of staff including support, sales and senior management as well as external consultant teams.
  • A history of building successful teams and businesses within the property industry including the ability to develop systems, procedures and structures.
  • Multiple Award winner including Colliers, UDIA and REI awards.
  • Extensive TV, radio and press experience.
  • Established relationships with key players across the property development industry including marketing, agency, planning, architecture, construction, lawyers, valuation, finance, journalists and public relations.
  • Intimate knowledge of NSW developments and good working knowledge of QLD and VIC markets.

Business and Educational Background

Curtis holds a Bachelor of Business (Land Economics) and is a licenced Real Estate, Strata Management and Business Agent. Curtis’ previous roles include National Director Residential Project Marketing with Colliers International, Sales & Acquisition Executive for Sunland Group, NSW General Manager of PRDnationwide Corporate focusing on Project Marketing and development services. Prior to PRDnationwide Curtis was Managing Director of the Sydney office of valuation and advisory firm Herron Todd White where he specialised in Residential Development Sites, 5 Star Accommodation Hotels and Residential Golf Resorts.
